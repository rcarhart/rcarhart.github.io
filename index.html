Hi, I am Ross Carhart. I am a dedicated data professional and consultant with a passion for leveraging data to solve complex business problems and drive measurable growth. Over my career, I've specialized in transforming messy, complex data into clear, powerful business insights that directly impact the bottom line. I thrive on collaborating with cross-functional teams to build robust, scalable data solutions. My work has involved partnering with major brands to uncover actionable marketing insights and automate reporting processes, resulting in millions of dollars in savings and increased efficiency. My expertise lies in modern data warehousing technologies, including Snowflake and Databricks. I focus on creating efficient data models and automating workflows, ensuring businesses have reliable, high-quality data when they need it. I believe data should be an accelerator for business growth, not a bottleneck.

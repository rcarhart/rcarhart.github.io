About Me

Hello and Welcome! I'm Ross Carhart, a seasoned data professional with over 6 years of diverse experience across multiple industries. My journey has led me to work with a wide spectrum of data types, ranging from genomic and financial to social media data. Skilled in an array of tools like SQL, Python, Tableau, and PowerBI, I've honed my expertise in various data frameworks, including data warehousing, data lakes, and data marts. My proficiency extends to different data processes, where I've tackled challenges in data cleaning, mining, and modeling. In the realm of data analytics, I've navigated through predictive, prescriptive, and descriptive analytics, providing comprehensive insights for decision-making. Moreover, my experience in data management encompasses vital areas such as data governance, security, and quality, ensuring robust and reliable data infrastructure. Join me in harnessing the full potential of your data to drive transformative business outcomes.

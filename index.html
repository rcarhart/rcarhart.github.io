About Me

As a University of Pittsburgh alumnus and former Division 1 soccer player, my journey uniquely intertwines athleticism and academic pursuit. In my initial venture into food science as a quality manager, I discovered a deep interest in analytical tools like Tableau, statistical process controls, lean manufacturing, and Six Sigma. This encounter sparked a fervent interest in data and programming, driving me to expand my educational horizons. Pursuing this passion led to acquiring a certificate in computer programming, a testament to my commitment to continuous learning. Professionally, my goal is to propel businesses toward unparalleled growth by integrating process automation and harnessing cutting-edge technology. The blend of my disciplined sports background and robust technical knowledge enables me to provide a unique perspective in this field. In my personal life, I find joy and balance in immersing myself in literature, spending quality time with my dog, exploring new cultures through travel, reconnecting with my soccer roots, and enjoying craft brews with friends.
